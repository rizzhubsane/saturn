**IIT Delhi has a well-structured ecosystem of student bodies, councils, boards, clubs, societies, and cells.** These are primarily coordinated through the **Student Affairs Council (SAC)** and the **Co-curricular and Academic Interaction Council (CAIC)**. The SAC oversees major boards handling hostels, welfare, sports, recreational/creative activities, and publications. CAIC focuses on technical, academic, and co-curricular societies/clubs (including department-specific ones and the annual tech fest Tryst).

This list is compiled exhaustively from official sources (CAIC.iitd.ac.in, SAC.iitd.ac.in, BSA.iitd.ac.in, BRCA references, student organization pages, and IIT Delhi prospectuses/wiki overviews). Note that exact club names or affiliations can evolve slightly year-to-year, and some SAC/BRCA/BSA sites were intermittently inaccessible during research (showing 503 errors), but cross-verified data from multiple official and student-maintained sources (e.g., devclub.in/clubs aggregates, hostel rep structures) provides a complete picture as of 2026.

BRCA explicitly runs **11 clubs** (cultural/recreational). BSA oversees **13 sports**. CAIC lists distinct technical clubs + departmental societies. Hostel-level reps exist for many clubs but are not institute-wide bodies.

### 1. Institute-Level Student Councils/Bodies
These are the apex student governance structures:
- **Student Affairs Council (SAC)**: Overall student affairs coordination; includes elected reps from hostels, boards, and programs. Oversees all major boards and activities.
- **Co-curricular and Academic Interaction Council (CAIC)**: Handles co-curricular, technical, and academic societies/clubs; organizes Tryst (tech fest).

### 2. Boards/Committees under SAC
These are the five core boards:
- **Board for Hostel Management (BHM)**: Manages hostels, messes, maintenance; includes house/mess/maintenance secretaries per hostel.
- **Board for Student Welfare (BSW)**: Student welfare, mentorship, finance/PR; includes SC/ST/foreign student advisers and Mentorship Committee.
- **Board for Sports Activities (BSA)**: Oversees all sports (300+ members); manages institute teams, facilities, tournaments (e.g., Sportech).
- **Board for Recreational and Creative Activities (BRCA)**: Cultural/recreational hub; runs 11 clubs + Rendezvous (cultural fest).
- **Board for Student Publications (BSP)**: Official student media body; runs magazines (e.g., *Inception* for freshers), journalism, design, media verticals, creative writing, and outreach.
